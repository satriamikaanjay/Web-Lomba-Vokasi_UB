Preserving Indonesia’s Green Heritage

Greenesia is committed to protecting Indonesia’s natural treasures from tropical forests and mangroves to plantations that sustain life. Together, we can safeguard our earth for a greener future.

Explore Green Maps

What is Greenesia?

Greenesia is a platform dedicated to environmental preservation in Indonesia. We provide information, interactive maps, and education about forests, mangroves, and plantations, bringing people closer to nature. Greenesia is not just about knowledge, but also a call to action to protect our earth together.

Learn More

Our Global Movement

At Greenesia, we believe that preserving nature is a shared responsibility. From Indonesia, we carry the spirit of sustainability to the world — connecting communities, sharing knowledge, and inspiring real actions for a better planet. Every small step in our archipelago contributes to global change.

Ministry of Forestry: 84,000 Hectares of Mangroves Rehabilitated Over the Last 5 Years

The Director of Mangrove Rehabilitation at the Ministry of Forestry (Kemenhut), Ristianto Pribadi, noted that 84,000 hectares of mangroves have been rehabilitated over the past five years. Meanwhile, the government has set a target of rehabilitating 600,000 hectares of mangroves by 2024. Ristianto also acknowledged that the forestry sector has not yet become a government priority program.
