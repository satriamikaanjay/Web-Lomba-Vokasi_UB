About Greenesia

We are a passionate team dedicated to empowering communities to map their local green resources and create a more sustainable future.

Our Mission

Greenesia is committed to providing a free, open, and accessible platform for everyone. We aim to help communities across Indonesia map sustainable resources, strengthen local engagement, and drive positive environmental and social change. By showcasing green maps, we believe individuals and organizations can make wiser and more sustainable choices.
